The Smart Moss Wall

Our research topic is called "The smart moss wall for reduce Carbon Dioxide and particulate matter ". We have chosen this topic due to rapidly growing of air pollution that influence our daily lives. Air pollution often associates with carbon dioxide and photosynthesis process. Because by burning of fire in oxide it becomes to CO2 also,therefore, this article will be focused on the smart moss wall in spite of the fact that there are much more appliances of Smart Technologies in our world.


Smart wall is the system that consists of many devices that are connected with each other by one network.Artificial Intelligence is a system that can make its own decisions without involving humans. Such a combination may be a powerful solution to many problems, including the ones from important fields like Healthcare, Business and Industry.


We examined the topic of communication technologies of the Internet of Things and looked at some information, including nano-technology, emission control, and rechargeable battery, as well as more specialized solutions such as Smart moss wall, solar energy, hydro energy.


Smart moss wall – smart wall of moss and other organics. Although there are a lot of living creatures, this only minimizes this process. Just to make such situations even easier, we found a way to make a smart wall 3.5 meters long out of moss with its own smart functions. It will not only absorb dirty substances and recycle, it will also analyze the area and transmit information about the environment to the performer. It will also be constantly powered by solar panels, and water will be used to maintain the work of the wall - specially stored in the tank.


Solar energy – Just a green energy which helps to decrease using carbon energy in our life.
Hydro energy – Same as solar energy, it helps to decrease using a burning energy in our life. Also it can be used as cooler system for some technologies.
